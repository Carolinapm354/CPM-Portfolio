Welcome to my Portfolio

This portfolio is a curated selection of my most significant works and research experiences, meticulously crafted to showcase my readiness and passion for pursuing a graduate degree in an interdisciplinary field. Drawing from a diverse range of projects and research endeavors, it offers a comprehensive view of my journey and areas of expertise. This portfolio represents my background in environmental design and is tailored for my application to MIT's Media Lab. It showcases a selection of my architectural works, research experiences, and other relevant accomplishments, highlighting my readiness for graduate studies in this innovative field.

It includes:

  • Architectural Works: My projects prioritize sustainability and resilience, incorporating features to withstand natural disasters.
  • Sustainability: My designs emphasize sustainability through energy-efficient practices, renewable materials, and green technologies.
  • Research Experience: I have conducted research on climate change adaptation, disaster-resilient architecture, and sustainable urban planning.
  • Natural Disasters: I've studied the impact of natural disasters on the built environment and developed strategies for minimizing risks.
  • Visual Communications: Proficient in visually communicating complex design concepts through various media.
