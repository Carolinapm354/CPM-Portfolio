Carolina Pérez is a senior in UPR-RP studying Architecture and minoring in RISE-UP. She focuses on designing resilience infrastructure and empowering vulnerable communities affected by phenomena. She is an ambitious latina woman studying a STEM field, who grew up on an island that has faced adversities. Experiencing Hurricane Maria and the January 2020 earthquakes, motivated her to search for climate change solutions, strategize for risk management, and contribute to community resilience. She is becoming a disaster-recovery architect, who conducts risk-targeted research and mitigates damages due to disasters. By working at research labs, she can further develop her knowledge and skills, including: team-working, critical thinking, architectural program knowledge, hardworking, and fast learning. She aims to improve her skills and contribute to significant change in the world.
